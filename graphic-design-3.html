Poster Project

2023 — Digital Composition / Visual Communication

This poster was created for CGT 118 at Purdue University as an exploration of digital composition and visual communication. I reimagined the classic film poster by blending iconic imagery with a contemporary layout, focusing on the themes of friendship and spontaneity. The project challenged me to combine photo editing, composition, and typography into a unified design that communicates the film's lighthearted tone.
