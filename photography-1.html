In this independent study project, I designed 3 unique packaging concepts for a fictional makeup company, each reflecting a different aspect of the brand identity. The project involved creating visually appealing and functional packaging designs that effectively communicate the brand's values and appeal to the target audience. This final deliverable includes detailed mockups of the packaging concepts, showcasing how the brand can be represented through physical products in retail settings.
