Still Life Photography

2023 — Composition / Studio Lighting

These photographs showcase versatility in mood, composition, and light manipulation by exploring studio lighting techniques. The left uses hard, directional lighting to cast elongated, sharp shadows, emphasizing clean geometry and a modern editorial aesthetic. The right uses a single soft light source and a black background to control reflections on glass and accentuate the textures of the flower and seashells. Together, they demonstrate technical skill concerning hard and soft illumination, prop styling, and intentional use of shadow to define form and depth.
