Packaging Photography

2023 — Composition / Packaging / Photography

These photographs were taken to showcase my packaging designs in a visually compelling commercial setting. I art directed each image by selecting props, constructing the set, and positioning products to guide the viewer's attention through the composition. Studio lighting was used to create mood and dimension while highlighting key packaging details. The resulting images balance product visibility with atmospheric storytelling.
