3D Modeling Project

2023 — Geometric Modeling

This 3D model was created as the final project for CGT 116 – Geometric Modeling for Visualization and Communication. The assignment challenged students to design and model a children's play table, but I expanded on the original concept by creating a piece that blends playful functionality with a sophisticated autumn-inspired aesthetic. The project demonstrates my ability to interpret a design brief while introducing my own creative vision through thoughtful modeling, composition, and material selection.

Software used: Autodesk Maya
