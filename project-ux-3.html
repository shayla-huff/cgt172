High Fidelity App Design Redesign & Re-Evaluation

2025 — Evaluation / Redesign / Re-implementation / Re-evaluation

For this project, we took our Project 2 high-fidelity prototype and ran a full usability + UX quality evaluation, then redesigned the prototype based on evidence, and evaluated it again. We recruited three target users who were unfamiliar with our concept (and not in our group) and conducted semi-structured interviews, documenting the process using the 7 interview stages discussed in class (Kvale). We also completed an observation study and a cognitive walkthrough, administered the System Usability Scale (SUS), and performed a subject matter expert (SME) audit using UX heuristics and the 13 Principles of Display Design. We then synthesized all findings into a prioritized list of recommendations and used them to redesign and re-implement the interface. As part of the redesign process, we used Figma Make (generative tools) to accelerate exploration, then manually refined the final hi-fi prototype—improving interactions, visual identity, and branding—and clearly documented what changed and why. Finally, we re-evaluated the updated prototype with the same three users using interviews, observation, SUS, and an updated SME reflection to verify improvements and validate the redesign decisions.
